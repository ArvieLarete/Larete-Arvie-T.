The Nature"

Nature can be seen as a home, resource, inspiration, or separate entity. It can be seen as a provider or a refuge. It is worshiped, feared, respected, and exploited. It is both part of us and beyond us. Despite our human influence, we remain dependent on nature for survival.



"Gabaldon Falls Nueva Ecija"


Gabaldon falls is a scenic waterfall in Nueva Ecija, Philippines, known for its clear waters and lush surroundings. It's a popular spot for hiking and swimming.



""Dingalan Aurora Butas na Bato"


Bukas na Bato in Dingalan. Aurora, is a natural rock formation with a large hole, located along the coastline.It offers stunning ocean views and is a popular spot for sightseeing, hiking, and photography
